Class Subjects

Astrology

Delve into your own astrological blueprint with classes of increasing complexity and self-awareness that will teach you how to read and create birth charts.

Gemstones & Crystals

Surround yourself with the energy that you desire by understanding the significance and resonance of natural gemstones and crystals.

Jewelry Making

Imbue your own style, soul, and energy into the j-ewelrythat decorates your being. Tara will teach you tricks on how to manipulate materials to create stunning artwork from your own energy.

Herbology

Learn an ancient understanding of nature and its relation to us through these Herbology courses. Discover simple cures for ailments, powerful spiritual energies, and take a glimpse into the lives of our ancestors

Palmistry

Every action that we make in our lives is displayed throughthe palms of our hands... Explore another blueprint ofyour personality and gain further perspecive and depth on who you are with Tara by learning how to read palms.

Tarot

Peek behind the veil, find meaning to your current circumstance, help understand what it is that your want and what it is that you need. Explore a vision of the metaphysical motion beyond our daily lives and learn to recognize and read this movement with our lead instructor Jesse.
